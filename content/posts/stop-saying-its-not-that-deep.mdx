---
title: "Stop Saying ‘It’s Not That Deep’"
excerpt: "Depth is not a vibe check. It’s a way of honoring the layers people bring to a story, a street, a room."
cover: "/images/notes/note-1.svg"
category: "Essay"
---

Sometimes “not that deep” is code for “I’m not ready to sit with this.” I keep returning to the idea that depth is a muscle. We can build it with practice, patience, and the courage to ask better questions. If a story takes time to unfold, let it. The city has always been deep; it’s our attention that fluctuates.
