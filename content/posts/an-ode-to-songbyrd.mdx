title: "An Ode to Songbyrd"
excerpt: "A love letter to baselines, neon, and the kind of listening rooms that make the city feel small."

Songbyrd was the kind of place where the floorboards hummed along. You could catch a riff in the hallway and still make it back for the chorus. This is a small archive of that glow, for anyone who keeps ticket stubs in their wallet.
